ZigZag Coasters

At ZigZag coasters, our mission is to combine functionality with sustainability, in order to create our jigsaw coasters!

We intend to use recyclable materials to produce our product, whilst maintaining art as a central element. We hope our product inspires consumers to treat our planet with more care.

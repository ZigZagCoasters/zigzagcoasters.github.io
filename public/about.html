About Us

We are ZigZag Coasters, a Young Enterprise team of 20 innovative students for the academic year 2024-25.

Our aim? To make stylish, creative, and eco-friendly coasters that make a statement.

We are a creative, collaborative and compassionate team, and every single coaster is made keeping quality and uniqueness on top. Whether you need something distinct, elegant, or personalized, we’ve got you covered.

Being in Young Enterprise isn’t just about a business, it is a place to learn, grow and challenge yourself. We are excited to take on this challenge together and bring you coasters that make an impact.

Follow our journey as we zigzag our way to success!

We are located at the Hollyfield School and Sixth Form Centre
